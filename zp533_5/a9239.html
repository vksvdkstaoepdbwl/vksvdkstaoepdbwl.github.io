【本の内容】
For undergraduate courses in Computer Business Engineering and Information Ethics at the junior/senior level.A highly flexible learning tool this collection of 38 case studies on information and computer ethics addresses the most salient ethical issues of the information age and illustrates the key concerns of computer specialists and information managers today. Engaging readers in an exciting discovery process as they work through the material it offers a practical balance for all levels of students - neither too technical for the novice in computer issues nor overly simplistic for those majoring in computer related subjects.
