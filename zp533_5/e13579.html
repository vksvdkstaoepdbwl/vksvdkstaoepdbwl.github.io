Set up your A6
1.Charging via a magnetic charging cable. The indicator light is red during charging. The charging completion indicator goes out.
2.Long press the right speaker for 3 seconds to turn on the pairing mode, and the hanging neck LED light flashes for 3 seconds.
3.Connect to mobile phone：Open the Bluetooth settings of the mobile phone, select "A6", hear the voice reminder "connect", the pairing is successful, and the hanging neck LED light flashes.

Multi-function touch button
Power on：Long press the right button for 3 seconds.
Power off：Long press the right button for 3 seconds.
Adjust volume:Long press the right button to increase the volume, long press the left button to decrease the volume.
Play/pause music:Click the left/right button.
Next song: Double-click the right button when the song is playing.
Previous song:Double-click the left button when the song is playing.
answer the phone:Answer the call when the headset is pressed once the left button or the right button.
hang up the phone:Hang up the call when the headset is pressed once the left button or the right button.
Reject call:Long press the left/right button for 2 seconds.
Wake up SIri:3 click the left button.
Hanging neck LED light switch:In the power-on state, 3 click the right button to close, and 3 click the right button again.
