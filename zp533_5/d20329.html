    In early 2017, Prism Corporation successfully launched the “Heady” multifunctional headlamp. We have since received amazing responses from customers and it inspired us to launch the Heady II!

    Heady II is equipped with warm and cool white in diffused light, with ultra-lightweight advantage. Our headlamp offers 350 lumens, 3 lighting modes ? focus/spot, cool white diffused, and warm white diffused.

    3 Lighting Modes
  • Spot/Focus Light (80m): Focused, concentrated bright light with long range distance
  • Flood/Diffused Light (Cool White): Diffused light to illuminate nearby areas with wide and even light range
  • Flood/Diffused Light (Warm White): Diffused light to illuminate nearby areas with wide and even light range (Max 175 Lumens)
