説明: Sesame mud is the mountain mud（本山段泥） more characteristic, and by many people like a mud.

The reason why it is called sesame mud, because the original mud made into works after firing, there are many white particles, red particles and black particles, natural densely covered in the body surface, is sesame shaped.

Sesame mud works of raw ore, the background color is green, slightly dark, black particles or red particles are very prominent. This is the result of burning ferric oxide at high temperature

The density of black particles in sesame mud is relatively large, the color is obvious, and the layers are clear. The overall background color and the layers of particles are relatively clear, just like the color points dotted on the light yarn. Although there are white particles in sesame mud, the number is small and the visibility is low.

Because of the large particles, strong sand texture and large porosity in the sesame mud, the air convection is smooth, the air permeability is good, and the adsorption is good. After a long time of soaking, the mud will become smoother and brighter. The iron content of the original sesame mud is higher than that of the general mountain mud. After prolonged potting, the particles will become dark red. If you use Pu "er tea and other black tea series to potting, the effect will be more obvious. So the raw sesame works tea, good for the body.
