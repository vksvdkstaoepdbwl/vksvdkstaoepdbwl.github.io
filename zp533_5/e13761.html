Introduction
This industrial-grade RS232 shield from DFRobot is specially designed to expand one RS232 port (D-Sub, 9Pin, male) with high reliability for LattePanda Alpha & Delta.

Features
Signal & power isolation
Reliable transmission
Up to 115200 bps communication baud rate
Easy to connect and use
No effect on the cooling performance of mainboard
DB9 adapter and copper columns and screws

Application
Facilitate the development and use of industrial-grade RS232 communication on Windows (compatible with Linux)

Specification
Operating Voltage: 5V
Operating Temperature: -40℃ to +85℃
Baud Rate: up to 115200 bps
Isolation Voltage: 2500VDC
EIA/TIA-232-F Standard Compliant
Not Support Hot Plugging
