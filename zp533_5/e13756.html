  • The Lilypad Sewable Electronics Kit for Arduino LilyPad lets you explore the wonderful world of electronic sewing (e-sewing) and e-textiles through a series of introductory projects using the LilyPad system.
  • You’ll learn how to sew basic circuits to light up LEDs, control them with buttons and switches and even make experiment with a LilyPad circuit that reacts to ambient light levels.
  • In addition to LilyPad LEDs and battery holders for Arduino , the kit comes with a LilyPad USB board that let you explore the circuit before you sew the pieces into a project.Each LilyPad component was creatively constructed with large sew tabs to allow for stitching into clothing. Various input, output, power and sensor boards are available.
  • This starter learning kit includes conductive thread bobbin (10m) and different color felt sheet(27*20.5cm), you could use it to make some small interesting doll as your designing.
