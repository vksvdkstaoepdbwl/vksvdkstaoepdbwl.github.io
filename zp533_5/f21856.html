California Dreaming: Movement and Place in the Asian American Imaginary

著者名: Christine Bacareza Balance; Lucy Mae San Pablo Burns; Russell Leong; David K. Yoo; Christine Bacareza Balance; Tracy Lachica Buenavista; Lucy Mae San Pablo Burns; Leilani Chan; Wendy Cheng; Dilan D"Lo Srijaerajah 出版者: University of Hawai"i Press 出版年: 30 Aug 2020 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackA multi-genre collection featuring works by Asian American artists based in California. Exploring the places of ""Asian America"" through the migration and circulation of the arts, this volume highlights creative processes and the flow of objects to understand the rendering of California"s imaginary.
