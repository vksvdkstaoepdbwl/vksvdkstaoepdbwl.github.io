A Year in Seventeenth-Century Kyoto: Edo-Period Writings on Annual Ceremonies, Festivals, and Customs

著者名: Gerald Groemer 出版者: University of Hawai"i Press 出版年: 31 Dec 2022 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackExplains Japanese conceptions of time and space within which annual celebrations took place and outline how ceremonies and festivals in and about Kyoto were chronicled, described, and interpreted from the earliest times to the seventeenth century. The final two chapters offer annotated translations of writings from the seventeenth century.
