The LINKUP Internal SAS SFF-8643 to U.2 SFF-8639 with SATA Power Cable makes it possible to
upgrade a computer to U.2 NVMe SSD speeds. Capable of performing 5x faster than its SATA SSD
counterparts, these cables allow the connection of U.2 NVMe SSDs even when a U.2 port is not
available.
